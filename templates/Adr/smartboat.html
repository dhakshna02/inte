Smart Boat IoT by ADR Group

The Smart Boat IoT application provides an integrated real-time boat monitoring solution for both business and personal use. The platform offers a range of features to ensure the safety, security, and efficient operation of various boats and floating vessels. Owners can remotely check their boat's status through IoT devices, mobile, and web applications

  • Battery Monitor

    ● Continuously monitors engine and house batteries.
    ● Alerts users of necessary actions to maintain battery health.
    ● Provides real-time updates on battery status, temperature, and abnormal conditions.
    ● Supports 12V, 24V, and 48V batteries with alerts for critical levels. Operates in temperatures from -20°C to +85°C.

  • Location Tracker

    ● Real-time GPS tracking and geo-fencing.
    ● Alerts for movement deviations and dock entry/exit.
    ● Visual trip logs for past journeys.
    ● Provides real-time updates, geo-fencing with a 1km boundary, and trip logs. Operates in temperatures from -20°C to +85°C.

  • Smart Fluid Monitor

    ● Monitors water levels in various tanks (drinking, freshwater, wastewater).
    ● Sends alerts for minimum/maximum water levels.
    ● Monitors various tank levels, sending alerts for levels below 20% or above 100%.
    ● Operates in temperatures from -20°C to +85°C.

  • Bilge Pump Monitor

    ● Continuously checks water levels in the bilge.
    ● Alerts for bilge pump activity and potential water accumulation issues.
    ● Tracks bilge pump activity, alerts for abnormalities, and operates in temperatures from -20°C to +85°C.

  • Security Monitor

    ● Detects motion and sends alerts for security breaches.
    ● Captures and sends photos when motion is detected.
    ● Live video surveillance and alarm activation for unauthorized access.
    ● Detects motion, captures images, triggers alarms, and provides live video feed.
    ● Operates in temperatures from -20°C to +85°C.

  • THS Monitor

    ● Monitors temperature, humidity, and smoke levels in critical areas.
    ● Alerts for temperature rises, gas leaks, and humidity changes.
    ● Monitors temperature, gas, and humidity with alert notifications.
    ● Operates in temperatures from -20°C to +85°C.

  • Smart Connect

    ● Remote control for various devices and appliances on the boat.
    ● Allows on/off switching for air conditioners, lights, refrigerators, and more via mobile app.
    ● Allows remote control of appliances, ensuring no conflict with manual operation.
    ● Operates in temperatures from -20°C to +85°C.

Testing and Reliability

Each module undergoes rigorous testing to ensure safe operation, accurate monitoring, and reliable alerts. Features are tested for proper integration with the mobile app, database storage, and real-time notifications to ensure seamless user experience and dependable boat management.

The ADR Group's Smart Boat IoT solutions offer comprehensive monitoring and control, enhancing safety, security, and efficiency for boat owners. For more information, visit our product page.
