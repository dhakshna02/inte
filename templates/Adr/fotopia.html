Photographer Calendar Integration

  • Integration

    Fotopia seamlessly integrates with photographers' calendars, synchronizing availability in real-time. This ensures the calendar is always up to date with the latest schedule changes, providing users with accurate booking options. Real estate professionals can confidently schedule photo shoots, knowing they have the most current information at their fingertips

  • View Availability

    Fotopia allows users to view a photographer's calendar directly within the app, showcasing available dates and times for booking. This feature streamlines the scheduling process, enabling real estate professionals to easily select and secure photo shoots, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience from start to finish.

  • Automated Notifications and Reminders

    Fotopia also features automated notifications and reminders, keeping both photographers and clients informed about upcoming photo shoots. These timely alerts help reduce the risk of missed appointments and ensure everyone is well-prepared, enhancing the overall efficiency and professionalism of the booking process.

Simplified Booking Process

  • Select Photographer

    Users begin by selecting their preferred photographer for the real estate photo shoot. This initial step ensures they choose the best fit for their project, based on availability and photographer profiles.

  • View Calendar & Choose Date and Time

    After selecting a preferred photographer for their real estate photo shoot, users can view the integrated calendar showing the photographer's availability. They can then browse through the calendar to find an available date and time that fits their schedule.

  • Block Calendar & Confirmation

    After selecting a suitable date and time from the integrated calendar, users can block the calendar to reserve the slot for their photo shoot, preventing others from booking the same time. The app then sends a booking confirmation to both the user and the photographer, detailing the scheduled photo shoot.
